About Toronto Cupcake

Toronto's leading destination for gourmet handcrafted cupcakes since 2010.

About Us

.............

Welcome to Toronto Cupcake! Founded in 2010, we are proud to be Toronto's leading destination for gourmet branded cupcakes. Whether you're celebrating a birthday, hosting a corporate event, or simply indulging in a sweet treat, our handcrafted cupcakes are made fresh daily using the finest ingredients.

We love the idea that our treats are making people happy every time they bite into one. How much fun is that!

At Toronto Cupcake, we believe in giving back to the community. Since our inception, we have partnered with numerous local organizations, donating our time and delicious cupcakes to support causes that make a difference in local and regional communities.

About Michelle

.............

Toronto Cupcake was created by Michelle Harrison to pursue her love of baking. Inspired by her mother, Michelle opened Toronto Cupcake in August 2010 as one of Canada's first gourmet cupcakeries.

Giving Back to Our Community

Toronto Cupcake is committed to making a positive impact — locally and globally.

Our Commitment

.............

Toronto Cupcake works with many neighbourhood and community organisations in the GTA. Since opening in 2010, Toronto Cupcake has been willing to assist by donating cupcakes, time, and caring to many charitable causes.

"We make a living by what we get. We make a life by what we give."

— Sir Winston Churchill

Land Acknowledgement

Toronto Cupcake acknowledges that Toronto operates on the traditional territory of many nations. We are committed to reconciliation and supporting Indigenous-led initiatives in our community.

Global Solidarity

Standing with Palestine 🇵🇸

Toronto Cupcake stands in solidarity with the people of Palestine. We believe in the dignity, safety, and humanity of all people. A portion of proceeds from select cupcake sales is donated to humanitarian relief efforts supporting families affected by the conflict in Gaza.

Local Initiative

GTA Food Bank Donations

Every week, unsold cupcakes are donated to local shelters and food banks across the Greater Toronto Area. Since 2010, we have donated thousands of cupcakes to families in need.

Charity Events

Community Bake Sales

We partner with local schools, mosques, churches, and community centres to host charity bake sales — raising funds for causes that matter to our neighbours.
